Welcome to the Frontier

✦

Covaya is a nation built by exiles on the far edge of the known world. They crossed the Strange Mountains through the Mandlewurd Pass and decided — against all good sense — to stay. That stubbornness is the defining quality of the Covayan character.

It has been twenty-five years since the Shadow Wars ended. The land is healing, slowly. The outlying settlements are being rebuilt. The High Chancellor in Copper City is working to stitch the nation back together. In small frontier towns along water ways, people are just trying to live.

But something is wrong. The strangeness in the south — in the mountains, in the desert, in old ruins nobody talks about — has always been there. It's getting worse.

The State of Things

The Shadow Wars ended a generation ago, but they left scars. Towns abandoned. Records destroyed. Governance fractured. The people who survived them are still rebuilding — and still, quietly, afraid of the dark.

The Stars Are Gone

Fifty years ago the stars simply stopped. No explanation that satisfied. The two moons remain — Nui and Mikro — and they are now the only lights in the night sky. Navigation, farming, faith — all of it has reorganized around their cycles.

The Gods, Such As They Are

The old gods are diminished, absent, or exposed as liars. New ones have risen — smaller, stranger, more present. The faith in gods has been broken, and people who focused their lives on them are few and far between.

Almost fifty years of starless nights. Two moons to navigate by. Through all the chaos, no clean answer has emerged.

Compasses fail. Distances misbehave. Whatever made the Strange Mountains strange did it long before anyone arrived.
