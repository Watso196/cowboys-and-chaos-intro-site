The Spines

The Spines are a jagged mountain range defining Covaya's northern interior — distinct from the Strange Mountains to the south in that their strangeness is geological rather than planar. They are simply difficult. The passes are high, the winters are hard, and the people who live in the settlements along their foothills have developed the particular self-sufficiency of people who cannot rely on help arriving in time to matter. Ata Tazan is the largest settlement in the region — a town with unusual access to the high passes into the interior and a reputation for people who prefer altitude to neighbors. Badwater Outpost, to the east, serves the edge of the Great Meadow and is the last reliable stop before the coast road north to Copper City.
