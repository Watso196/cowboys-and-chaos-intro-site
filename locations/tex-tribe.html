The Tex Tribe

The Tex moved into desert territory during the Shadow Wars — into land the settlers had abandoned and left to the strangeness of the south. Where the Wort stayed in the jungle they had always occupied, the Tex moved toward the mountains, toward the strange places, toward the ruins of the Before People in the foothills. Their knowledge of the desert is unmatched. They know the water sources, the seasonal routes, the things that move in the desert at night that deserve respect. What drove them south during the Shadow Wars is one of the more carefully kept mysteries in Covaya. Outsiders who ask receive answers that don't quite answer the question. The Tex framed their departure not in terms of the wars but in terms that have not been translated clearly into common — and the Wort, who stayed behind, frame it in terms that suggest the split was about something larger than geography.
