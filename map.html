Copper City
Capital — Northeastern Coast

The seat of the Covayan Compact. The largest harbor on the continent. The place where power lives, where deals are made, and where the High Chancellor has been trying to stitch a nation back together for twenty-five years.

Frontier Town — Snake River

Where the campaign begins. A hundred people, one tired sheriff, one bar, one trading post, and a river that runs strange from the mountains. The bandito problem is getting worse. Everyone knows it.

The Wort Tribe
Tribal Territory — Northern Jungle

The Wort have occupied the northern jungle longer than settler records reach. The jungle is their boundary and the settlers learned early not to push through it. Their knowledge of the forest is unmatched.

The Tex Tribe
Tribal Territory — Great Tex Desert

The Tex moved into desert territory during the Shadow Wars — into land the settlers abandoned. They know the desert the way the Wort know the jungle. What drove them south is one of Covaya's more carefully kept mysteries.

The Strange Mountains
Dangerous Region — Southern Border

Compasses fail. Distances misbehave. Whatever makes these mountains strange made them that way long before the Shadow Wars. The Before People's ruins cluster in the foothills. The Snake River flows out of here and carries something with it.

Grand Mesa
Region — Central West

A vast elevated plateau between the Wort Woodlands and the Great Tex Desert. Mesa Grand sits at its edge — a settlement that occupies a position between the two worlds of this continent and has learned to navigate both.

Region — Northern Mountains

A jagged mountain range defining Covaya's northern interior. Ata Tazan sits in its foothills — a settlement with unusual access to the high passes and a reputation for people who prefer altitude to neighbors.
