Copper City

Copper City began as a camp at the outlet of the great northern river — a place where fresh water, coastal access, and nearby copper deposits made settlement unusually viable. It grew quickly. The mines drew people. The harbor drew trade. By the end of the Pioneer Period it was the largest settlement on the continent, and it has remained so ever since. It is the seat of the Covayan Compact, home to the High Chancellor, the Chancellery Court, and the most consistent exercise of Covayan law anywhere on the continent. It also has the highest concentration of followers of Alithe — the cracked hourglass appears on politician lapels and merchant ledgers, worshipped by people who may not fully understand their god's precarious position, or may not care.
