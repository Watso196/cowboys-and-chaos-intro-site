The Strange Mountains

The Strange Mountains are not merely difficult terrain. They are genuinely strange: compasses fail, distances misbehave, and travelers who have made the passage describe effects that suggest the Shadowfell's proximity on the mountain range is not a recent development. Whatever made the Strange Mountains strange made them that way long before the Shadow Wars. The Mandlewurd Pass — the only reliable route through the range — is how the first settlers came to Covaya. The crossing was not safe then and is not entirely safe now. Before People ruins cluster in the foothills: worked stone and metal built in styles that predate any known tradition, abandoned without clear evidence of violence or disaster. They were simply left. The ones nearest the mountains have a reputation for making people uncomfortable in ways they struggle to articulate. The Snake River flows out of the mountains and carries something with it. Locals in Spine Gulch have their rules about what to do when it does.
