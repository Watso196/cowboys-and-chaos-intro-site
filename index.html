Welcome to the Frontier

✦

Covaya is a nation built by exiles on the far edge of the known world. They crossed the Strange Mountains through the Mandlewurd Pass and decided — against all good sense — to stay. That stubbornness is the defining quality of the Covayan character.

It has been twenty-five years since the Shadow Wars ended. The land is healing, slowly. The outlying settlements are being rebuilt. The High Chancellor in Copper City is working to stitch the nation back together. And in the small frontier towns along the desert river routes, people are just trying to live.

But something is wrong. The strangeness in the south — in the mountains, in the desert, in the old ruins nobody talks about — it never fully went away. It's getting worse.

The State of Things

The Shadow Wars ended a generation ago, but they left scars. Towns abandoned. Records destroyed. Governance fractured. The people who survived them are still rebuilding — and still, quietly, afraid of the dark.

The Stars Are Gone

Fifty years ago the stars simply stopped. No explanation that satisfied. The two moons remain — Nui and Mikro — and they are now the only lights in the night sky. Navigation, farming, faith — all of it has reorganized around their cycles.

The Gods, Such As They Are

The old gods are diminished, absent, or exposed as liars. New ones have risen — smaller, stranger, more present. And in the unstable south, something that has no name yet is filling the vacuum they left.
